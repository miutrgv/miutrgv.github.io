Target-Aware Molecule Generation with Diffusion
and Reinforcement Learning Optimization

This project focuses on generating molecules with improved binding affinity to specific target proteins, a critical aspect of drug discovery. The approach combines Equivariant Graph Neural Networks (EGNNs) with Denoising Diffusion Policy Optimization (DDPO). Reinforcement learning is applied to optimize the diffusion process, enhancing molecular properties and contributing to the development of more effective therapeutic compounds.
