Virtual Screening Drug Discovery

Virtual Screening Drug Discovery refers to a range of technique used to automatically search and evaluate very large libraries of compounds database to select a small number for biological testing. If 3D structure of the target is known, then structure-based methods is used otherwise ligands-based method is used. One of the first steps in the structure-based methods is identifying druggable binding site on target protein. We are working on 3D convolutional neural network to predict druggable protein binding site. We are using an annotated database of druggable binding sites sc-PDB to train and evaluate our neural network.
