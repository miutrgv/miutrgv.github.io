Virtual Screening Drug Discovery refers to a range of technique used to automatically search and evaluate very large libraries of compounds database to select a small number for biological testing. If 3D structure of the target is known, as shown in the 3D model above (4OR0 transport protein), then a structure-based method can be used otherwise a ligands-based method is used. One of the first steps in the structure-based methods is identifying druggable binding site on target protein as shown in the image below.

We are working on 3D convolutional neural network to predict druggable protein binding site. We are using an annotated database of druggable binding sites sc-PDB to train and evaluate our neural network.
