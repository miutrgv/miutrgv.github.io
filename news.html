ECE Poster Competition

February 23, 2022

Congradulations to Christian Aguilar, Michael Cancino, Stephany Lopez, and Allyssa Villanueva for winning the 2022 Undergraduate Electrical and Computer Egineering Poster Competition at UTRGV!

Their project "End-to-end Translation System for American Sign Language" presenting the first steps toward utilizing deep learning to translate ASL earned them 1st place in the competition. Christian, Michael, Stephany, and Allysa all worked on their project under the guidance of UTRGV ECE and CS faculty Dr. Dongchul Kim, Dr. Weidong Kuang, and Mr. Carlos Rodriguez-Betancourth.
