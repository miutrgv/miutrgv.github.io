Research Lab

Our research lab is equipped with a dynamic array of resources, our facility has multiple high-performance computers with RTX-3080 GPUs, ensuring unparalleled computational power for various tasks. Additionally, our GPU cluster, with 4 GTX-1080 GPUs, provides optimized parallel processing capabilities, complemented by our HPC Center's cluster with 32 A100 GPUs, ideal for intricate simulations and data-driven research. Beyond computing prowess, our lab houses a 3D printer, for experimentation and prototyping in the realm of three-dimensional creations.

Lab Resources:

  • Multiple GPU Computers
  • GPU Cluster
  • HPCC Cluster
  • 3D Printer

Multiple GPU Computers

GPU Cluster

Training data sets on a single GPU can sometimes take hours or days depending on the size of the data set. When data sets are too large, our researchers turn to our GPU Cluster.

Cluster Specs:

  • GPU: NVIDIA GeForce GTX-1080
  • Total GPUs: 4
  • Memory (RAM): 128 GB
  • Storage: 1 TB

HPCC Cluster

When the GPU Cluster is not enough, our researchers turn to the HPC Center's cluster. Our lab has access to all of the cluster's SXM nodes.

Cluster Specs:

  • SXM Nodes: 4
  • Node-to-Node Bandwidth: 200 Gb/s

SXM Node Specs:

  • GPU: NVIDIA A100 Tensor Core
  • Total GPUs: 8
  • Memory (RAM): 640 GB
  • GPU-to-GPU Bandwidth: 600 GB/s

3D Printer

For fast and precise three-dimensional prototypes our researchers turn to our AnkerMake M5 3D Printer.

Printer Specs:

  • Print Precision: 0.1 mm
  • Print Volume: 235x235x250 mm^3
  • Built-In AI Camera Monitoring
