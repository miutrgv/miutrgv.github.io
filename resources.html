Research Lab

The Machien Intelligence (MI) lab provides a synergistic environment for students to conduct their research utilizing meachine learning methods primarily reinforcement-learning. Our lab is equipped with an array of GPU workstations made available to all MI members. In addition, MI members have access to our GPU Cluster within UTRGV's High Performance Computing (HPC) center.

GPU Cluster

In a GPU cluster, multiple GPUs collaborate to divide and conquer complex computations, significantly accelerating processing times compared to traditional CPU-based systems.

Cluster Specs:

  • SXM Nodes: 4
  • Node-to-Node Bandwidth: 200 Gb/s

SXM Node Specs:

  • GPU: NVIDIA A100 80GB
  • Total GPUs: 8
  • Memory (RAM): 640 GB
  • GPU-to-GPU Bandwidth: 600 GB/s
